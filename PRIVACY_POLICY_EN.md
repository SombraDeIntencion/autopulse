# AutoPulse Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 11, 2026

## 1. Introduction

AutoPulse ("we", "our" or "the application") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our automotive workshop management mobile application.

**Important:** AutoPulse does NOT collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial or advertising purposes. All data collected is exclusively for the internal operation of the application and remains under your control.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you create an AutoPulse account, we collect:
- **Email:** For authentication and account recovery
- **Username:** For experience personalization
- **Password:** Stored encrypted via Firebase Authentication
- **Local biometric data:** If you enable fingerprint login (processed locally on your device)

### 2.2 Workshop Information
For application functionality, we store:
- **Vehicle data:** Make, model, year, plates, VIN, color, mileage
- **Customer data:** Vehicle owner name (provided by you)
- **Service orders:** Technical information, diagnostics, performed work
- **Photographs:** Vehicle and work images (voluntarily captured)
- **Documents:** Quotes, reports, and technical documentation

### 2.3 Technical Information
We automatically collect:
- **Device data:** Device model, operating system version
- **Usage logs:** Access dates, features used (only for technical improvements)
- **Performance data:** Error and crash information to improve stability

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data **exclusively** for:

### 3.1 Service Operation
- Authentication and access control to your account
- Storage and management of vehicle information
- Creation and tracking of service orders
- Report and documentation generation

### 3.2 Security
- Protection against unauthorized access
- Identity verification via biometric authentication (optional)
- Fraud detection and prevention

### 3.3 Service Improvements
- Bug fixes and error correction
- Performance optimization
- New feature development

**AutoPulse does NOT use your data for:**
- ❌ Targeted advertising
- ❌ Sale or exchange with third parties
- ❌ User profiling for marketing
- ❌ Behavior analysis for commercial purposes

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Infrastructure
All data is stored on **Google Firebase**, a Google Cloud platform with certification:
- ISO 27001 (Information Security)
- SOC 2/3 (Security Controls)
- Compliance with international data protection standards

### 4.2 Security Measures
We implement multiple security layers:
- **Encryption in transit:** HTTPS/TLS for all communications
- **Encryption at rest:** Data stored with AES-256 encryption
- **Robust authentication:** Email verification and optional biometric authentication
- **Access rules:** Firestore Security Rules ensuring only you can access your data
- **Image compression:** Automatic size reduction to optimize storage

### 4.3 Data Location
Firebase servers may be located in different geographic regions. For more information about Firebase data location, see: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## 5. Sharing Information with Third Parties

### 5.1 Third-Party Services Used
AutoPulse uses the following third-party services **exclusively** for technical operation:

**Firebase (Google):**
- Firebase Authentication: Account management
- Cloud Firestore: Database
- Firebase Storage: Image storage
- **Privacy Policy:** https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

**No other third parties have access to your data.**

### 5.2 Legal Exceptions
We will only share your information if:
- Required by court order or competent authority
- Necessary to protect legal rights or security
- You provide explicit consent

## 6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

### 6.1 Access and Portability
- **View your data:** Access all information stored in your account
- **Export data:** Request a copy of your data in readable format

### 6.2 Correction and Deletion
- **Modify data:** Edit incorrect or outdated information
- **Delete account:** Request permanent deletion of your account and data

### 6.3 Privacy Control
- **Biometric authentication:** Enable/disable at any time
- **App permissions:** Control camera and storage access from device settings

## 7. Data Retention

### 7.1 Active Data
While your account is active, we will maintain your data to provide the service.

### 7.2 Account Deletion
Upon deleting your account:
- All your personal data will be **permanently** deleted within 30 days
- Backups will be deleted in the next scheduled cycle (maximum 90 days)
- Deletion is **irreversible**

### 7.3 Anonymous Data
We may retain aggregated anonymous technical data (without personal identification) for:
- General usage statistics
- Performance improvements
- Error reporting

## 8. Minors

AutoPulse is designed for professional use in automotive workshops. We do not intentionally collect information from individuals under 18 years of age. If we discover that we have collected data from a minor without parental consent, we will delete that information immediately.

## 9. Application Permissions

AutoPulse requests the following permissions on Android:

### 9.1 Required Permissions
- **Internet:** Communication with Firebase for data synchronization
- **Storage:** Temporary saving of images before uploading

### 9.2 Optional Permissions
- **Camera:** Vehicle photo capture (only when you use this feature)
- **Biometric:** Fingerprint authentication (only if you enable it)

**Important:** You can revoke these permissions at any time from Android settings. Some features may not be available without corresponding permissions.

## 10. Contact

To exercise your rights, resolve questions, or report privacy issues, contact us:

**Email:** privacy@autopulse.app  
**Expected Response:** 48-72 business hours
